Life supporting equipments are essential for the survival of critically ill patients. These machines provide necessary support and care to patients when their organs or body systems are failing. Dialysis machine is used for patients with kidney failure, while ventilators are used for patients with respiratory failure. ECG machines are used to diagnose and monitor heart conditions, and defibrillators are used to restore heart rhythm in cases of cardiac arrest. Oxygen concentrators are used to provide oxygen to patients with respiratory distress. All of these machines work together to save lives and provide critical support to patients in need
